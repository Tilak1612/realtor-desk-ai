## Brand Voice

### Tone
- **Professional but approachable** — sounds like a successful agent talking to another agent
- **Confident** — we're backed by real results, not hype
- **Action-oriented** — every headline pushes toward a next step
- **Empathetic** — we know agents are busy; acknowledge that before pitching

### Writing Rules
1. Use "you" and "your" — speak directly to the agent
2. Short sentences. Short paragraphs. Agents scan, they don't read walls of text.
3. Lead with outcomes: "Close more deals" before "AI-powered lead scoring"
4. Use specific numbers: "Save 8 hours/week" not "save time"
5. Avoid: "leverage", "synergy", "cutting-edge", "next-generation"
6. Prefer: "automate", "built for agents", "never miss a lead", "close deals faster"
7. Bilingual note: FR copy must feel natural — not a direct translation of EN

## Visual Direction

- **Primary Color**: #ea580c (orange-600 — warm, energetic, stands out in real estate)
- **Accent Color**: #1e293b (slate-900 — grounding, professional)
- **Background**: #ffffff / #f8fafc (clean white and slate-50)
- **Typography**: Inter for body text; bold Inter for headings
- **Style**: Clean, minimal, lots of whitespace, rounded corners (radius-lg), card-based layouts
- **Imagery**: Lifestyle photos of real Canadian agents at work — not generic stock. Action shots preferred.
- **Icons**: Lucide React icons (consistent with shadcn/ui)
