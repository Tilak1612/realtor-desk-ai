# Security Policy for RealtorDesk AI

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in RealtorDesk AI, please report it to:

**Email:** security@realtordesk.ai

Please include:
- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- Potential impact
- Suggested fix (if any)

We take security seriously and will respond within 48 hours.

## Security Measures

### Data Protection
- All data encrypted at rest and in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Canadian data residency
- PIPEDA compliant
- Regular security audits
- SOC 2 Type II (in progress)

### Authentication
- Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
- Two-factor authentication (2FA) available
- Session management with automatic timeout
- OAuth 2.0 support

### Compliance
- PIPEDA (Personal Information Protection)
- CASL (Canadian Anti-Spam Legislation)
- GDPR compliant
- Regular compliance reviews

### Infrastructure
- DDoS protection
- Regular backups
- Disaster recovery plan
- 99.9% uptime SLA

## Responsible Disclosure

We believe in responsible disclosure and will:
1. Acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
2. Provide a timeline for resolution
3. Keep you informed of progress
4. Credit you for the discovery (if desired)

## Out of Scope

The following are not considered vulnerabilities:
- Social engineering attacks
- Physical attacks
- Denial of service attacks
- Spam or phishing attacks

We are working on establishing a bug bounty program. Stay tuned!
